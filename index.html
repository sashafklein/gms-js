Gimme Sats

Dead-simple Lighting payment

A library for effortlessly integrating Lightning payment into web apps

Give it a try!

Fully customizable

Easily set defaults, themes, and fixed amounts or notes

Tick the boxes to edit component options and customize the payment flow for the below GimmeSats button:

amount (Prefill amount to $20.)
note (Set pre-filled note.)
amountIsFixed (If amount is unchecked, will throw error.)
noteIsFixed (If note is unchecked, will be blank.)
theme (Set white theme.)
to (Change recipient.)
displayName (Customize recipient text.)

Open-source and service-agnostic

Accepts payment from any Lightning wallet. Soon to invoice using the service of your choice
